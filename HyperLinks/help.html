FAQ

How do I create a new blog post?

To create a new blog post, simply click on the "New Post" button in the dashboard. You can then add a title, content, and any necessary media. Once you've finished, click on "Publish" to make your blog post live.

How do I edit an existing blog post?

To edit an existing blog post, click on the "Edit" button next to the post in the dashboard. You can then modify the title, content, and any media associated with the post. Once you've made your changes, click on "Update" to save them.

How do I delete a blog post?

To delete a blog post, click on the "Delete" button next to the post in the dashboard. You will be prompted to confirm your action. If you're sure you want to delete the post, click "Confirm Delete" to remove it from your blog.
